Introduction to Python Programming

Introduction to Python Programming" is a comprehensive guide designed for novice computer programming students seeking an intuitive entry into the world of Python. Distinct in its approach, this book not only embodies a unique philosophy but also stands out through its focused content, varied activities, topic development, and meticulous attention to programming intricacies. Tailored with precision, the book's content is meticulously curated by analyzing the Python course syllabi of esteemed universities in the USA, Europe, and Asia. Acknowledging the diverse prerequisite knowledge levels of students, the authors strategically address the challenges of teaching and learning, drawing from their extensive experience with diverse student cohorts. Setting itself apart from conventional programming texts, this book employs natural language expressions instead of the cryptic jargon often associated with the programming domain. With a primary objective of ensuring accessibility, the authors aim to bridge the gap between students' learning experiences and the practical application of their newfound knowledge.

About Author

Dr.Gowrishankar S. is currently working as Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Dr.Ambedkar Institute of Technology, Bengaluru, India. He earned his PhD in Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, India in 2010 and MTech in Software Engineering and BE in Computer Science and Engineering from Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, India, in the years 2005 and 2003 respectively. From 2011 to 2014 he worked as a senior research scientist and tech lead at Honeywell Technology Solutions, Bengaluru, India.
